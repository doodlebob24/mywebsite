Fortnite Battle Royale

Fortnite Battle Royale is a PvP game where you and 99 other players fight for the Victory Royale. You can customize your skin, have fun with different emotes, and build an amazing base that cannot be destroyed. This game is extremely popular, and there are more than 125 million global players, with 40 million people logging on every month.

This is what the Fortnite map looks like. You can land Anywhere, just beware of the storm. As the game goes On, the storm closes in. As the storm closes in, it does more damage, so don't get caught in the storm!
